Indie EdTech and The Personal API

I wanted to pull together a toolbox that I could use throughout the conversations I would be having at Davidson College this week, that would help folks understand what an APIs, their relationship to the web and mobile world we depend on each day.

Let's look at the world of APIs around you:

  • Why APIs? - You are using APIs every day to pay for things at the store, to taking pictures with Instagram, and sharing on Facebook, all the way to doing your online banking. The more you understand this world of APIs, the more successful you will be in your personal, and professional career--able to take control over more of your digial presence.
  • What is a website? - A website is a folder on a computer that is plugged into the Internet, that is given an Internet address, with a domain or subdomain that allows anyone with the address to find it on the Internet--let's explore the basics of what is a website >>
